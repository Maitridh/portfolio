About

Design is
my language

I'm Maitri Dharmik, a graphic designer and visual artist from Jabalpur, India. I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science while building a creative practice that spans poster design, album art, automotive graphics, and digital branding.

My work lives at the intersection of street culture, music, and visual storytelling. I believe every design should carry emotion — something raw, something that stays with you. I work in Photoshop, Canva, Figma, and Framer to bring ideas to life.

Available for freelance projects, collaborations, and full-time creative roles. Let's make something unforgettable.
